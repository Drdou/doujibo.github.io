Dr. Jibo Dou is a faculty member at the School of Engineering, Hangzhou Normal University（杭州师范大学工学院）, where he serves as a Lecturer (since December 2025). He is primarily engaged in research on pollution environmental geochemistry, with strong research interests in the environmental behavior and effects of free radicals, remediation of soil and water organic pollution, and biogeochemical cycling of biogenic elements.

Dr. Dou obtained his Ph.D. in Soil Science from the College of Environmental and Resource Sciences, Zhejiang University, in April 2024(浙江大学环境与资源学院土壤学博士) . Prior to his current position, he worked as an Assistant Researcher (August 2024 – December 2025) and as a Laboratory Assistant (April 2024 – July 2024) at the same university. He also completed a joint master's training program at the College of Chemistry, Nanchang University (September 2017 – December 2018)（南昌大学化学学院联合培养硕士）. He earned his M.E. in Environmental Engineering from the College of Urban and Environmental Sciences, Northwest University (August 2016 – June 2019)（西北大学城市与环境学院环境工程硕士）, and his B.E. in Environmental Engineering from the School of Materials and Chemical Engineering, Xi’an Technological University (September 2012 – July 2016)（西安工业大学材料与化工学院环境工程学士）.
